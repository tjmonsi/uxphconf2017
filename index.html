About UXPH

The Biggest Conference on User Experience in the Philippines

 

Frustrated over bad experiences like long queue lines, traffic, inconsiderate services and terrible product design? Want to do something about it? Want to help design for a better Philippines?

In 2014, we brought speakers like Whitney Hess in Manila to talk about User Experience Design and the Design Career path. In 2017, we would like to make it more meaningful for you as we bring speakers on topics like:

  • Data-driven Visual Design
  • Designing Services and Products for the Public Sector
  • Product Design for Start-ups
  • Application of User Experience and Human-Centered Design Concepts to domains such as Finance, Healthcare, and others

Speakers

Jennifer P. Teves, PhD

 

Human Factors Research Scientist based in the Bay Area and Twin Cities. She has over 10 years of experience in cognitive science research focusing on aviation technology, human factors in healthcare, enterprise solutions, automation and controls, and data visualization.

Brian Caoyonan

 

Senior User Experience Consultant at IAG. He was previously a User Experience Lead in NBN telecommunications Australia.

Ben Kim

 

UX Researcher and Designer based in Los Angeles. His focus is on the end-to-end product experience, starting with research and taking the feature all the way to development. Previously worked with UXPin and Lexus
